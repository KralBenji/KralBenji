About Me

I didn't plan on making music a career, and I still don't. If thats where this journey takes me, I am all for it. But to be clear, this is just a passion I love. I will continue to put my heart, mind, soul, and body into making music that I love. I started making music as a way to vent what was on my mind, which I will never stop, but as I grow as an artist and grow in my faith I will continue to use it as a way to reach and inspire people.
